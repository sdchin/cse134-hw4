About

Hi! My name is Steven Chin. I'm an aspiring web developer based in San Diego and soon-to-be graduate from UC San Diego (c/o 2023) with a B.S. in Mathematics-Computer Science.

As a chronically online individual, I love learning about how the Web works. Whether I'm watching cooking videos on YouTube or playing League, I'm always curious about the technology that enables the everyday things we use.

My main skills lie in front-end development, though I'm also versed in backend languages. I'm interested in UX/UI design and helping make the Web more accessible for all.

In my free time, you can find me trying out a new recipe for my food page or out and about shooting film. I'm also an avid fragrance collector and fashion enthusiast.

Please feel free to contact me on my socials or using the contact form on this website. Looking forward to talking soon!
